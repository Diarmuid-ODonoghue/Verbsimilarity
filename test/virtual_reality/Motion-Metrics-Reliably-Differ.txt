The Fundamentals of Endovascular & Vascular Surgery (FEVS) is a curriculum that includes an endovascular model for skills testing, which aims to differentiate between competent and non-competent performers. The aim of our study is to further validate the model and test its reliability in assessing the performance of endovascular trainees in an uncontrolled setting.
