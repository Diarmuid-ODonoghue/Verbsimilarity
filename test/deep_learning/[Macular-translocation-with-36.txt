During surgical extraction of choroidal neovascular membranes (CNV) in age-related macular degeneration (AMD), the defective foveal retinal pigment epithelium (RPE) is removed. Subsequent translocation of the foveal neural retina to adjacent healthy RPE should result in stabilization and possibly improvement of visual acuity.
